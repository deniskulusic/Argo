FAQ

When is the best time to rent a cruiser?

The ideal cruising season in Croatia stretches from spring to autumn. The pre-season runs from Easter until early July, featuring variable weather, sea temperatures up to 23°C, longer days, and fewer crowds at marinas and anchorages. July and August mark peak season, offering warm sea temperatures, air temperatures exceeding 30°C, lively atmospheres, and bustling marinas, perfect for guests looking for nightlife and vibrant activities. September and October are excellent for those seeking romantic, quieter experiences with pleasant weather, fewer crowds, shorter days, and generally lower prices, similar to the pre-season period.
